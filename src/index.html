About the App

"Zuma's Revenge" is a captivating and adventurous marble shooter game where players take on the role of a fearless ball-shooting frog. Set in a lush jungle, the game involves shooting colored balls from the frog's mouth to match and eliminate a chain of advancing balls before they reach the skull. The game challenges players with various levels, each with different patterns and speeds of the ball chain, as well as obstacles and power-ups. It combines skill, strategy, and speed, making it an exciting game for all ages. **
